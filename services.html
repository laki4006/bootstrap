Aircraft Repair Service

At Aviatia, we offer comprehensive aircraft repair services to ensure the safety and optimal performance of your aircraft. Our highly skilled technicians and state-of-the-art facilities allow us to handle a wide range of repairs, from minor maintenance tasks to major structural repairs.

  • Engine repairs and maintenance
  • Avionics troubleshooting and upgrades
  • Airframe repairs and inspections
  • Component replacements and repairs
  • Sheet metal repairs and fabrication

We prioritize quality workmanship, strict adherence to industry standards, and timely completion of repairs to minimize downtime for your aircraft. Trust us to deliver reliable and efficient repair solutions for all your aviation needs.

About

Founded in 1973 by aviation enthusiasts, Aviatia has grown to become a leading aerospace company, offering a comprehensive range of services including aircraft maintenance, charter flights, pilot training, and aviation consulting. With a strong commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, Aviatia continues to uphold its reputation as a trusted partner in the aviation industry.
